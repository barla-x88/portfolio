ABOUT

I've always been captivated by the world of computers and their remarkable ability to simplify tasks for humans. My journey into the vast landscape of coding began with C++, where I crafted small console applications that ignited my passion for programming. The turning point came when I stumbled upon AutoHotKey, a tool that opened up a realm of possibilities for automating tasks, including manipulating the Windows registry on Windows. It was a game-changer, allowing me to streamline processes and enhance efficiency. Diving deeper into the coding universe, I explored VB6 and created a string conversion application. This tool seamlessly transformed quoted-printable strings into plain-text strings, unlocking the ability to read and store notes from my phone on my computer. Around the same time, I discovered mobile site builders. This introduction led me to the foundational elements of web development: HTML tags and CSS. The elegance of crafting web applications captured my heart, and I found myself immersed in the art of building digital experiences.
